Well met, traveller. I'm Justin – writer, builder, and hopeful dreamer.
Born in the early '80s, I grew up in small-town Indiana. Books
like _The Hobbit_ and _The Eye of the World_ showed me the power of words to
conjure entire worlds to explore and fed a slow-burning fire inside my heart.

By day I work as a software developer — designing, building, and maintaining
backend services. By night – when I'm not juggling my three children – I
explore the quieter inner world of my imagination: [writing](#writing) fiction,
[building](#projects) open-source software, and [examining](#blog) our world
to try and understand why injustices exist and what can be done to right them.

While I prefer to write fantasy, I do occasionally dip my toes into science
fiction, horror, pulp noir, and other genres. If you like what you see,
[say hi](#contact) or share my writing with a friend. If you don't, say hi
anyway. I'm always up for a chat about tabletop role-playing games, writing,
or whatever else is worth talking about.
