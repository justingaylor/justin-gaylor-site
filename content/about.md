Well met, traveller. I'm Justin – writer, builder, and hopeful dreamer.
Born in the early 80's, I grew up in small-town Indiana in the United
States. Works of epic fantasy like _The Hobbit_ and _The Eye of the
World_ showed me the power of words to conjure entire worlds and
led to a lifelong interest in world-building and storytelling.

By day I work as a software developer – designing, building, and maintaining
backend services. By night – when I'm not juggling three children – I explore
the quieter inner world of my imagination: [writing](#writing) fiction,
[building](#projects) software, and [examining](#blog) the world around me.

While I prefer to write fantasy, I do occasionally dip my toes into science
fiction, horror, pulp noir, and more. If you like what you see,
[say hi](#contact) or share my writing with a friend. If you don't, say hi
anyway. I'm always up for a chat about tabletop role-playing games, writing,
or whatever else is worth talking about.
