Hail, traveller. I'm Justin – writer, builder, and something in between.
I came of age in the '80s, when computers filled rooms with the soft green glow of
cathode rays and imagination did the rest. Games like _Ultima_ and _Wizardry_ taught me
that a few lines of text could conjure entire worlds. That lesson never left.

By day I practice the craft of software — assembling tools, solving problems,
contributing to the open source commons. By night I inhabit a quieter guild:
stories, essays, and the slow patient work of putting something true on the page.

This site is a collection of my work, a place to share what I've made and what I'm making.
If you like what you see, say hi or share it with a friend. If you don't, say hi anyway. I'm always up for a chat about tabletop role-playing games, writing, or whatever else is worth talking about.
